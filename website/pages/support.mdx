# Support

## Common Issues

### Can't Access Study Materials

If you're having trouble accessing the study materials, please ensure you have a stable internet connection. If the issue continues, feel free to reach out to us at [admin@learnverse.live](mailto:admin@learnverse.live) for assistance.

### Broken Links

If you come across any broken links while browsing our site, we would appreciate it if you could let us know. You can report them by visiting our [Contact](https://learnverse.live/contact) page.
